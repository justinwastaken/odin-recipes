Spaghetti De La Hood Recipe

Description

Spaghetti de la Hood is a large bowl of Spaghetti containing Meatballs; Spaghetti made like in da Hood! We recommend serving it with the Wine 19 Crimes Cali Red.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground sirloin
  • 1 cup dry Italian bread crumbs
  • 1 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for serving
  • 2 tsp salt, plus more for salting water
  • ½ tsp cracked black pepper
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 4 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 2 (28-oz) cans whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 (6-oz) can tomato paste
  • 1 lb Spaghetti
